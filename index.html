About Me

My name is Inga Locmele, and I am a frontend web developer with over 3 years of professional experience. Currently, I work remotely for Delta4 Digital Inc., a digital marketing company based in Ontario, Canada.

At Delta4 Digital Inc., our primary focus is building websites for clients across Canada and the United States, utilizing Tymbrel CMS as our foundation. We provide a comprehensive range of services, including both template-based and fully custom-built websites.

Last year, Delta4 Digital Inc. launched over 500 websites with just two frontend developers. This achievement highlights the exceptional productivity and expertise of our frontend development team.

In addition to my frontend skills, I have a solid background in database administration and information management. With over 10 years of experience in this field, I have also gained valuable leadership abilities by leading a team of 10 employees.

Furthermore, my passion for photography greatly enhances my ability to work with diverse UI designs. This hobby has honed my eye for visual aesthetics, composition, and attention to detail, enabling me to create visually captivating and user-friendly interfaces.

The combination of technical skills and creative sensibility derived from my photography background sets me apart in delivering exceptional frontend experiences.
